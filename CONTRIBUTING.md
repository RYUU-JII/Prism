# Contributing

Thanks for your interest in Prism.

Prism is currently in its early stages of development. To maintain a consistent architectural vision and focus on the core roadmap, **we are not accepting Pull Requests at this time.**

However, your feedback is invaluable! We are actively preparing for the **v0.2.0 update** and would love to hear from you.

### How to contribute
1.  **Report Bugs**: If you find a bug, please open an issue using the **Bug Report** template.
2.  **Suggest Features**: Have a great idea for a new feature? Use the **Feature Request** template.
3.  **v0.2.0 Feedback**: Share your thoughts on the new Glow Bar design and theme sync in the **Feedback** issue.

Thank you for helping us make Prism better through your insights and suggestions!
